Explore the pioneers and legends of early hip-hop

Artist Profiles

DJ Kool Herc

The Father of Hip-Hop

Pioneered the breakbeat technique and hosted the first hip-hop party in 1973.

Grandmaster Flash

DJ Innovator

Revolutionised DJing with cutting, scratching, and mixing techniques.

Afrika Bambaataa

The Godfather

Founded the Zulu Nation and defined hip-hop's four core elements.

Run-DMC

Kings of Rock

First hip-hop group to achieve mainstream success and platinum status.

Public Enemy

Political Missionaries

Brought political consciousness and activism to hip-hop music.

N.W.A

Gangsta Rap Pioneers

Revolutionised rap with raw, unfiltered street narratives from Compton.

LL Cool J

Ladies Love Cool James

First solo rap artist to achieve commercial mainstream breakthrough.

Rakim

The God MC

Transformed rap lyricism with complex rhyme schemes and platinum delivery.
